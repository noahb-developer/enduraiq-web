How to find your LTHR

30-minute field test:
1. Warm up for 15 minutes
2. Run or ride all-out for 30 minutes (solo, steady pace)
3. Your average HR during the LAST 20 minutes ≈ your LTHR

Do this separately for running and cycling — they typically differ by 5-10 bpm.
